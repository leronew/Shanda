Fun, sun and safety collide

Splash Ventures is located in Frigate Bay, one of the most popular beaches in St. Kitts. We offer endless fun-in-the-sun with our large, inflatable aqua park, suitable for swimmers and non-swimmers, ages 5 years and up. Come soak up some of our Caribbean sun on our safe and family-friendly fun park. Enjoy a variety of options ranging from swings, water trampoline, slides, brides and more. Our certified team in both safety and Customer Service looks forward to sharing in some of your most memorable moments captured on our beautiful island. Come splash with us where sun, fun and safety collide.
