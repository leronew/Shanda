Frequently asked questions
What are your guest policies?
  • All participants must sign a liability waiver.
  • A Parent or guardian must sign the waiver for participants under the age of 18 years.
  • Guest who are pregnant, had recent surgery, has back, neck or knee problems or any other serious health conditions should not participate.
  • All participants must wear a life vest provided by Splash Ventures in order to enter the aqua park.
  • Life vests should not be removed until the tour/session is complete.
  • All participants must participate in the short orientation and safety session before entering the aqua park.
  • No sharp or pointy objects should be left in pockets or clothing that would be worn on the aqua park.
  • No shoes or clothing with zippers or pointy objects should be worn on the aqua park.

If I cancel my booking will I get a refund?
  • We recommend guests contact us at least 24 hours before their tour or scheduled pick up time to cancel. We do offer refunds for all canceled tours made within this time frame.

  • 5 years

  • 230lbs or 135kgs
